Committees have been formed for the development, cooperation and problem solving of the students in the college.

Notice:Committees have been formed in the college. 13/08/2023

Committees are often formed in colleges and educational institutions to serve various purposes
and address specific needs. Here are some common reasons why committees are formed in colleges:

Event Planning: Committees may be created to organize and manage various events within the college, such as graduation ceremonies, orientation
programs, conferences, seminars, and workshops. These committees ensure that events run smoothly and effectively.

Faculty and Staff Recruitment: A committee might be formed to handle the recruitment and selection process for new faculty and staff members.
This committee typically screens applications, conducts interviews, and recommends candidates for hire.

Overall, committees play a vital role in the effective functioning of colleges by providing a structured way for various stakeholders,
such as faculty, staff, students, and administrators, to collaborate, make decisions, and address important issues.
